About me

I remember the day when I first saw the computer, it was very exciting. Out of excessive curiosity, I blocked my first mobile phone SIM card, the very first day I got it. I can't think of a good reason why I didn't choose computer science as my university degree. I guess back then I thought that, I couldn't have fun while working.

In the math exam I got 52 out of 55. Solving difficult tasks gives me a great sense of accomplishment. Every job I worked in, eventually led me to google sentences: "how to create a website" or "If I learn several computer languages will I be a polyglot?". So, I decided to learn programming. This time I want to try teamwork, I think it will be more productive and compelling for me.
